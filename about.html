Our Story

We Started Because
Bihar Deserved Better

Finding a property in Bihar was broken. Fake listings, exploitative brokers, zero transparency — families were losing time and money every day.

Zorario was founded in Gaya with a simple mission: make every property listing verified, every transaction transparent, and every family's property dream achievable — whether they're in Patna, Begusarai, Munger, or any of Bihar's 38 districts.

Today, with 10,000+ verified listings and 5,000+ happy families served, we're just getting started.

Our Values

What We Stand For

These principles guide every decision we make at Zorario.

100% Verified

Every listing on Zorario is personally verified by our team. We go to the property, check documents, and confirm details before it goes live.

Zero Commission

We never charge hidden fees or broker commissions. Property owners and seekers connect directly — keeping more money where it belongs.

Instant Access

Browse thousands of real properties instantly on your phone. No waiting, no calls to middlemen — just direct access to verified listings.

Hyper-Local

We're not a national platform pretending to serve Bihar. We live here, we know every district, and we're built specifically for Bihar's property market.

Local Support

Our support team speaks Hindi and English, understands local property norms, and is available round the clock to help you.

Built for Bihar

From Gaya to Bhagalpur, Patna to Purnia — every feature we build is designed with Bihar's people, culture, and property needs in mind.
