Introduction

Welcome to Zorario ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate Bihar's No.1 real estate platform at www.zorario.com and the Zorario mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the platform.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect data that is necessary to provide you with the best property-finding experience in Bihar.

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated automatically when you use our services:

  • Account Information: Name, phone number, and optionally email address when you register.
  • Property Listings: Property details, photos, pricing, and location information submitted by property owners.
  • Search & Usage Data: Search queries, filters applied, properties viewed, and time spent on listings.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (with your permission) to show nearby properties. We never store precise real-time location history.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our in-app contact features.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Zorario platform and app
  • Match property seekers with relevant verified listings
  • Send you property alerts and notifications you have opted into
  • Verify property listings submitted by owners
  • Respond to customer support inquiries
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and misuse of our platform
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations under Indian law
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for unrelated advertising.

Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Property Owners/Seekers: When you express interest in a property, your contact details may be shared with the relevant property owner, and vice versa.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for database and authentication, and may use analytics tools — all bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority in India.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred with appropriate notice.

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any unaffiliated third parties for commercial purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • User authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication with secure tokens
  • Database access is restricted by Firebase Security Rules
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments

While we implement robust security measures, no digital system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and not share your account credentials.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to initiate this.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from notifications at any time through app settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at +91 93342 98441 via WhatsApp or through our Contact page.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website (zorario.com) uses minimal cookies for:

  • Session management and user authentication
  • Remembering your language preference
  • Basic analytics to understand page visits (aggregated, not personal)

The Zorario mobile app does not use browser cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Children's Privacy

Zorario is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting a notice on our website and app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Sending a push notification to app users for material changes

Your continued use of Zorario after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
